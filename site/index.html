HPMOR Formalization Field Notes

This is not a roadmap. It is a record of where reality refused to cooperate.

We took claims from Harry Potter and the Methods of Rationality, translated them into definitions and theorems, and then asked what changed under formalization. Some claims survived intact. Some required assumptions the prose did not state. Some turned out to be examples wearing the costume of theorems. This page tracks those updates.
