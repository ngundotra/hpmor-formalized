HPMOR Formalization Field Notes

This is not a roadmap. It is a record of where reality refused to cooperate.

We took claims from Harry Potter and the Methods of Rationality, translated them into definitions and theorems, and then waited for mathematics to object. Sometimes it objected politely. Sometimes it produced a hidden assumption and dropped it on the desk like contraband. This page is the resulting incident log.
