# Physics

HPMOR Chapters: 2, 4-6, 28-30, 59, 78

## Goal

Formalize HPMOR's claims about how magic interacts with physical laws —
particularly conservation laws, symmetry principles, and the constraints
that real physics would impose on magical phenomena.

## What's here

| File | What it does | Tier | Finding |
|------|-------------|------|---------|
| Conservation.lean | Models conservation laws as invariants of discrete dynamical systems. Proves magic can break one conservation law while preserving another. | 2-3 | #11 |

## Findings so far

- **Conservation**: Harry jumps from "Transfiguration violates energy conservation" to "Transfiguration violates physics." But breaking one conservation law (energy) doesn't break all of them. Magic could preserve a modified conserved quantity under a different symmetry group. This is not an obscure technicality — it's how real physics works (parity violation breaks one symmetry while preserving others).
